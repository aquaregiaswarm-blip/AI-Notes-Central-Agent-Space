# Day-of Checklist

## Week Before
- [ ] Confirm attendee list
- [ ] Send prep instructions to participants
- [ ] Confirm catering
- [ ] Lab environments provisioned
- [ ] Materials printed

## Day Before
- [ ] AV equipment tested
- [ ] Network/WiFi confirmed
- [ ] Backup plans for common failures documented
- [ ] Contact info for all presenters

## Morning Of
- [ ] Arrive 1 hour early
- [ ] AV final check
- [ ] Sign-in sheet ready
- [ ] Name tags ready
- [ ] Lab credentials ready to distribute

## During
- [ ] Someone on "tech support" duty
- [ ] Track timing vs agenda
- [ ] Note issues as they happen

## After
- [ ] Collect feedback
- [ ] Retro meeting scheduled
- [ ] Thank you emails sent
